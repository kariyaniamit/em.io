Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Exam Matters ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our Android application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you register and use the Exam Matters app:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and city/district when you register.
  • Device Information: A unique device identifier (Android ID) to associate your account with a single device for security purposes.
  • Purchase Information: Records of batches or content you have purchased through Google Play Billing.
  • Usage Data: Content you access, PDFs you view, and videos you watch within the app.
  • Firebase Authentication Data: Login credentials managed securely by Google Firebase Authentication.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your user account.
  • Provide access to purchased educational content (batches, PDFs, video lectures).
  • Restrict account usage to a single registered device to prevent credential sharing.
  • Process payments and verify purchase status via Google Play Billing.
  • Send push notifications about new batches, content updates, and announcements.
  • Respond to support requests, including device reset requests.
  • Improve the app experience and fix technical issues.

3. Device Binding & Account Security

To protect our content and ensure fair usage, each Exam Matter account is linked to a single device upon first login. This means:

  • Your unique device ID is stored securely in our database.
  • Logging in from a different device will be blocked until your device is reset by our support team.
  • If you change your phone or perform a factory reset, please contact our support to unlock your account for a new device.

4. Content Protection

All PDF documents and video content within the app are protected. The app uses Android's FLAG_SECURE feature, which prevents screenshots and screen recordings of protected content. This is done to protect intellectual property rights of our educators and content creators.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Google Firebase Firestore — for user profiles, purchase records, and device information.
  • Google Firebase Storage — for PDF documents and educational materials.
  • Google Firebase Authentication — for secure login and session management.

All data is stored on Google's secure cloud infrastructure. We do not store your passwords — authentication is handled entirely by Firebase.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following cases:

  • Google Firebase / Google Play: As required to operate our services (authentication, storage, notifications, payments).
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Exam Matters, our users, or others.

7. Push Notifications

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send you push notifications about:

  • New batch releases
  • New content added to purchased batches
  • Important app announcements

You can disable push notifications at any time from your device's notification settings.

8. In-App Purchases

All purchases are processed through Google Play Billing. We do not store your payment card details. Purchase records (product ID, purchase token, acknowledgment status) are stored in our database to verify your access to purchased content.

9. Children's Privacy

The Exam Matters app is intended for students preparing for competitive examinations and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Request a device reset if you change your phone.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

11. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g. purchase records).

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us:

  • App: Exam Matters
  • Email: infoexammatters@gmail.com

By using the Exam Matters app, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
